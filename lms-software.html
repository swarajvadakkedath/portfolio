Project Overview

A user-first Learning Management System designed for schools that makes course creation, scheduling, assignment management, and performance tracking simple and accessible for non-technical teachers and students. The platform focuses on clarity and ease-of-use while offering the robust features schools need.

Objectives

  1. Make complex workflows (course creation, scheduling, assignments, grading) easy and fast for teachers.
  2. Create a simple, engaging experience for students to access lessons, submit work, and view progress.
  3. Provide clear performance tracking and reporting for teachers and school admins.
  4. Keep the product feature-rich but not overwhelming — prioritise progressive disclosure and contextual help.

Challenges

  1. Many features required (scheduling, bundle/course creation, assignments, grading, reports) threatened to overwhelm users.
  2. Teachers and students have wide variation in tech comfort—from confident digital users to practically first-time users.
  3. Need to support both quick actions (create a session in 30 seconds) and detailed workflows (rubrics, grade policies).
  4. Maintaining consistency and discoverability across modules so users always know where to go next.

Solution

  1. Persona-driven design: teacher & student personas and mapped primary journeys (create course, schedule a class, submit assignment, view progress).
  2. Progressive disclosure: default screens show the most common actions (Add Course, Schedule Session, Upload Assignment) with an “Advanced” toggle for power features.
  3. Modular composer for course creation drag-and-drop content blocks (video, PDF, quiz, assignment) with inline help and templates for common course types.
  4. Clear performance dashboard: teacher-facing class overview and student-facing progress card with visual indicators (completion %, latest grade, missing work).

Results

  1. Teachers can now create standard courses and schedule sessions with minimal clicks, reducing friction and training time.
  2. Students report an easier submission experience and faster access to materials.
  3. Platform ready to scale across classes and grades while staying consistent and accessible.
