Inventory Management Software

The Challenge

One of the major challenges in building the school store management software was creating a flexible and scalable system that could handle the complex needs of educational institutions.
Each standard (grade) required its own unique set of study materials, grouped and sold as bundles. These bundles needed to be fully customizable—store admins had to be able to create new bundles by combining individual items, and easily edit, add, or remove items at any time.
Another key challenge was supporting schools with multiple stores, including a central (main) store and one or more sub-stores. It was essential to enable real-time communication and inventory syncing between stores, along with the ability to transfer stock from the main store to any sub-store seamlessly.
Designing a system that maintained data accuracy, allowed easy bundle creation, and supported multi-store logistics was a significant technical hurdle that influenced the software’s core structure.
